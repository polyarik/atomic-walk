Atomic Walk - kinda mini-game; small practice with vectors

Gameplay:
+Move mouse to move the Hero
+Press rmb to plant a bomb
+Press lmb to detonate all planted bombs
+If Hero in blow area, he'll lose hp
+Damage depends on distance between hero n explosioned bomb
-If Hero hp decrease to 0, he'll die ◄

Graphics:
+Hero is a circle (color depends on his hp)
+Bomb is a grey-blue circe
+Bombs' affecting areas show as red alpha circles
.Hp bar consists of squares (located in top-left corner)
